Who We Are

Guardians of the
Horn of Africa's Seas

homebe.org — the Horn of Africa Marine Environment & Blue Economy organisation — is a regional NGO dedicated to protecting marine ecosystems and developing sustainable blue economies across Djibouti, Eritrea, Ethiopia, Kenya, Somalia, Somaliland, Sudan, and Tanzania.

We work with coastal communities, governments, and international partners to build a future where thriving oceans and thriving people go hand in hand — because healthy seas are the foundation of life in the Horn.

Ocean First

Marine health drives every decision we make.

Long-term Vision

Lasting change, not short-term fixes.

Community Led

Local fishers and communities lead the way.

Science-Driven

Evidence-based marine research guides our work.

What We Do

Featured Programs

Marine Conservation

Coastal Marine Protected Areas

Establishing and managing MPAs across the Horn's coastline, working with local communities to restore coral reefs, mangroves, and critical fish nurseries.

Learn more →
Blue Economy

Sustainable Fisheries & Livelihoods

Supporting artisanal fishing communities with sustainable practices, market access, and blue finance to build dignified, ocean-compatible livelihoods.

Learn more →
Climate Resilience

Mangrove & Coastal Restoration

Community-led mangrove restoration as a nature-based solution to coastal erosion, storm surge, and blue carbon sequestration in the Horn of Africa.

Learn more →
Ocean Governance

Marine Policy & Governance

Building regional frameworks for ocean governance, helping HoA governments develop national marine spatial plans and implement international ocean law.

Learn more →
Education

Ocean Literacy & Marine Education

Running marine science education programs for youth, training the next generation of HoA ocean champions, researchers, and conservation leaders.

Learn more →

Partner With Us

We partner with governments, UN agencies, research institutions, and the private sector to scale marine conservation across the Horn of Africa.
